Experiences

Student Council 2022 - 2024

During my time in high school, I applied and became a member of the Student Council. On my first year, I became a member in the Academics Department, and on the next, became the Vice President. Working in the Student Council had many challenges, but it was definitely a rewarding experience.

Yayasan Pondok Kasih Internship

In December 2023, IPH Schools had a two-week internship program where students would be equipped with real-world experience. I was stationed in Yayasan Pondok Kasih where I assisted the teachers during the morning lessons and even had several teaching moments in the evenings.

EcosySTEM_org

In January 2024, I was invited by a friend to join a student-led organization called EcosySTEM_org, which focuses on making scientific resources more accessible. Currently, I am the Physics Committee Leader, handling the posts and uploads of physics related content in @ecosystem_org.
